Spring. In football world Spring means pressure, nerves, injuries, finals, winners and loosers. Spring is a period of the year where past few months of hard work can reward you or brake you. It is this time of the year when the football season is finishing up, when Cup Semi-Finales and Finals and happening. Many teams and players are at the peak of their form and doing their best to get the most out of the current season so they can enjoy their summer holidays with Gold medal hanging on their chest.
